## 2. How Will It Be Sustainable Long-Term?

### A. Technical Sustainability

**Keep Technology Updated:**
- Regular updates and improvements
- Move to cloud hosting for better performance
- Add new features based on user feedback
- Maintain security with regular audits

**Make It Reliable:**
- 99.9% uptime guarantee
- Daily automatic backups
- Fast loading (under 2 seconds)
- Works on all devices and browsers

---

### B. Financial Sustainability

**How We'll Make Money:**

**Option 1: Free + Premium Model**
- Basic version: FREE for small institutions
- Premium version: $500-$2,000/year (advanced features)
- Enterprise version: $5,000+/year (custom features, dedicated support)

**Option 2: Pay Per User**
- $0.50-$2 per student per month
- Universities pay based on number of users
- Annual discounts available

**Option 3: One-Time License**
- $5,000-$20,000 one-time payment
- University hosts it themselves
- Annual support fee for updates

**Recommended: Hybrid Approach**
- Free basic version (builds adoption)
- Paid cloud-hosted version (convenience)
- Customization and training services
- Support contracts

**Projected Income:**
- Year 2: $50,000 (10 universities)
- Year 3: $200,000 (30 universities)
- Year 5: $1,000,000 (100 universities)

**Costs:**
- Developers: $30,000/year
- Servers and hosting: $10,000/year
- Support and marketing: $30,000/year
- **Total**: ~$70,000/year

**Break-even**: Need 50-100 paying universities

---

## 5. Risks and How We'll Handle Them

### Risk 1: Universities Don't Want to Pay
**Solution**: 
- Offer free tier to get them started
- Show clear cost savings (no printing, less admin time)
- Prove value with pilot programs
- Flexible payment plans

### Risk 2: Competition from Big Companies
**Solution**:
- Focus on what we do better (local, affordable, simple)
- Build strong relationships with customers
- Keep innovating and adding features
- Partner with local organizations

### Risk 3: Technology Problems
**Solution**:
- Regular testing and monitoring
- Backup systems and disaster recovery
- Security audits every 6 months
- Keep technology updated

### Risk 4: Not Enough Money
**Solution**:
- Start small, grow gradually
- Apply for grants and competitions
- Seek investors when we're proven
- Keep costs low (lean operations)
